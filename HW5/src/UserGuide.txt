How to play the game:

1) Go to your run configurations:
2) Make sure the tester class is set to "tester.Main"
3) Your program argument should be, "ExamplesZType"
4) Launch the game!
5) Type the first word that appears on your screen
6) The word you will be typing will ALAWYS be the word that is at the utmost bottom of your screen
7) You can see what you have typed next to "Typed"
8) If you mistype, you can delete letters and continue!
